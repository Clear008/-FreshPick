Fresh Pick

Fresh Pick is a user-friendly web application designed to streamline your grocery shopping experience. Select and purchase fresh fruits and vegetables online, then pick them up at your convenience.

Features

Freshness Guarantee

We ensure that every piece of produce delivered to your doorstep is farm-fresh and handpicked to meet our highest quality standards. If it's not fresh, it's free.

Wide Variety of Products

Choose from an extensive selection of fruits, vegetables, and organic produce. Whether you're looking for seasonal specials or everyday staples, we've got you covered.

Time-Saving

We save you time by gathering all the fresh products you need, ready for pickup at your convenience.

About the Developers

Hi, we are three passionate software engineering students with a keen interest in web development. The inspiration for Fresh Pick came from our shared frustration with the challenges of grocery shopping, especially during busy times and in the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic. We realized that many people, including ourselves, faced difficulties finding fresh, high-quality produce without the hassle of crowded stores and long lines.

Our journey began during a casual conversation where we shared our experiences and discussed potential solutions. This led us to the idea of creating a platform that ensures convenient access to farm-fresh produce, delivered right to your doorstep or ready for easy pickup. We wanted to make grocery shopping a seamless and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Fresh Pick is not just a project for us; it's a culmination of our learning and growth as software engineers. This project allowed us to dive deep into full-stack development, honing our skills in Django, MySQL, and responsive design. It challenged us to think creatively and work collaboratively, pushing the boundaries of our technical abilities.

This project is also part of our portfolio for Holberton School, where we have been trained to become proficient and adaptive software engineers. You can learn more about Holberton School and their innovative approach to software engineering education here.

We are excited to share Fresh Pick with you and hope it makes your grocery shopping experience more convenient and enjoyable. Thank you for supporting our journey!
