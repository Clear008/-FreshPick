Project Overview

Fresh Food

Fresh Pick is a user-friendly web application designed to streamline your grocery shopping experience. Select and purchase fresh fruits and vegetables online, then pick them up at your convenience.

About the Developers

Hi, we are three passionate software engineering students with a keen interest in web development. We created Fresh Pick to solve the problem of inconvenient grocery shopping and to learn more about full-stack development. Through this project, we enhanced our skills in Django, MySQL, and responsive design.
