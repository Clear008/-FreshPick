Fresh Pick

Online Grocery Shopping Made Easy

Project Overview

Fresh Pick is a user-friendly web application designed to streamline your grocery shopping experience. Select and purchase fresh fruits and vegetables online, then pick them up at your convenience.

Technology Stack:

  • Django: for rapid development and pragmatic design.
  • MySQL: for reliable data management.
  • Tailwind CSS: for custom, responsive designs.
  • HTML: for structuring and presenting content.
